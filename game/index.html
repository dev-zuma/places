How to Play - Detective Guide

Your Mission

Track criminal suspects across the globe using detective skills and geographic clues. Our enhanced cases require identifying 3 crime scene locations + 1 final hideout location where the suspect was captured.

Investigation Process

Turns 1-5: Identify 3 crime scene locations
Turns 6-7: Deduce the final hideout to capture the suspect

Available Clues: Evidence photos, geographic distances, timezone differences, thematic connections, and breakthrough discoveries. Clues vary by turn and difficulty.

Detective Tools & Tips

Investigation Journal: Use Timeline and Locations views to organize evidence.

Check Answers Tab: Submit location guesses and track your progress.

Evidence Photos: Click any image for full-size details. Look for specific geographic features.

Timer: Complete within 5 minutes for maximum score. Progress saves automatically when signed in.

Leaderboard & Scoring

Weighted Scoring: Easy (1x), Medium (1.25x), Hard (1.75x) multipliers reward challenge level.

Performance Bonuses: +75 points for solving first 3 locations in ≤5 turns, +100 points for 6th turn final location.

Leaderboard Access: Sign in to track your detective record and compete with other players across difficulty levels.

Rankings: Overall Champions, Easy Champions, Medium Masters, and Hard Heroes categories.
